daun teh grade I :
• 25.000
naik 5% ( + 1250)
• 26.250

.daun teh grade II
• 20.000
turun 15% ( - 3000)
• 17.000

perbandingan : awal = akhir

25.000A + 20.000B = 26.250A + 17.000B
( 20.000B - 17.000B) = (26.250A - 25.000A)
3000B = 1250A
A / B = 3000/1250
A/B = 12/5

Simple past tense adalah kalimat yang menunjukan keterangan waktu di masa lalu. Berikut adalah formulanya:

(+) Subject + V2

I went to the cinema last week

She met her husband 10 years ago

(-) Subject + did NOT + V1

She didn’t finish the exercise yesterday

I didn’t go to school last week

(?) Did + Subject + V1?

Did you see me yesterday?

Did Bella take her medicine?

Simple past tense of to be, to have, to do

I, He/She/It => was, had, did

You, We, They => were, had, did

Simple present adalah tense yang menunjukan kegiatan yang terjadi saat ini (present) maupun kebiasaan yang terjadi pada waktu tertantu secara rutin. Berikut adalah formulanya:

(+) Subject + V1 (-s, -es, -ies)

He goes to school

My daughter lives in South Africa

It snows a lot during the winter

Subject: (He, She, It)

(-) Subject + does/do NOT + V1

My mother doesn’t speak good english

It doesn’t rain in the summer

She doesn’t study Math on Monday

Subject: (Do => I, You, We, They) (Does => He, She, It)

(?) Does/Do + Subject + V1

Does he go to school?

Does she live in Paris?

Do you like apple?

Subject: (Do => I, You, We, They) (Does => He, She, It)

31. 8! - 7³ + 10Note : Grade 4 atau Grade 5 = BA​


Jawaban:

8! - 7³ + 10

= (8 × 7 × 6 × 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1) - (7 × 7 × 7) + 10

= 40.320 - 343 + 10

= 39.977 + 10

= 39.987

Pembahasan :

Faktorial.

Faktorial adalah mengalikan bilangan yang di akhir Dengan 1.

Contoh :

5! = 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 = 120

Perpangkatan.

Perpangkatan adalah perkalian antara suatu bilangan dengan bilangan itu sendiri atau bisa disebut perkalian berulang ulang.

Contoh :

5³ = 5 × 5 × 5 = 125

Penyelesaian dan Jawaban :

8! - 7³ + 10

= (8×7×6×5×4×3×2×1) - (7×7×7) + 10

= (56×6×5×4×3×2×1) - (49×7) + 10

= (336×5×4×3×2×1) - 343 + 10

= (1.680×4×3×2×1) - 343 + 10

= (6.720×3×2×1) - 343 + 10

= (20.160×2×1) - 343 + 10

= (40.320×1) - 343 + 10

= 40.320 - 343 + 10

= (40.320-343) + 10

= 39.977 + 10

= 39.987

Hasilnya adalah 39.987
