2. LANGKAH MEMBUAT ROTI DALAM BAHASA INGGRIS


How to Make a Quick Homemade Bread

Ingredients

2 cups very warm (not boiling hot) water

4 teaspoons instant yeast

1 tablespoon sugar

1/4 cup vegetable oil

5 cups flour

1 1/2 teaspoons salt

Part One of Three:
Making the Dough


1

Pour the water into a bowl. It's important that you use water that's very warm, but not too hot! Hot water could kill the yeast. Warm water will wake it up without killing it, which is what you want - that way the yeast will help the bread rise.[1]



2

Add the yeast and sugar. Use a spoon to stir it all together. The yeast will begin to react with the sugar, and the mixture will become bubbly and foamy. This should happen within just a few minutes.If 3 minutes have passed and nothing has happened, your yeast might be stale, and you may need to use another batch.You could also try again using water that's a little warmer or cooler, depending on what temperature you initially tried.


3

Pour the flour into a big mixing bowl. Five cups will yield two loaves of bread. You can use either all-purpose flour or bread flour. The bread flour might have a bit more height, but all-purpose flour works just fine, too.


4

Add the oil, salt and yeast mixture. Pour them one at a time over the flour.



5

Mix the dough. Use either a mixture with a dough hook attachment, a hand mixer or a wooden spoon to mix all the ingredients until they come together in a big, sticky ball of dough.

Part Two of Three:
Rising and Kneading



1

Put the ball of dough in an oiled bowl. You can wash out the mixing bowl and oil that one, or use a separate oiled bowl. It should be at least twice the size as the ball of dough, so the dough has room to rise.



2

Cover the dough and put it in a warm place. Cover itloosely with plastic wrap - it shouldn't be airtight - or place a clean dishcloth over the bowl. Put it in a warm area in your kitchen. If your kitchen tends to be drafty, preheat your oven to 200 degrees, turn it off, and put the bowl in there. It'll be just the right temperature for the dough to rise.



3

Let the dough rise for 25 minutes. During this time it will start puffing up. It won't quite double in size, but it will rise enough to give the dough a good texture.



4

Punch and knead the dough. If you have a stand mixer, use the dough hook attachment and knead the dough until it relaxes - about 5 minutes. If you don't have a mixer, you can knead the dough by hand. Turn it out onto a floured surface and use the heels of your hands to knead for 10 minutes, or until the dough relaxes.You'll know the dough has relaxed when it stops pulling back into a ball shape when you pause your kneading. It should be loose and malleable.The dough will also start to look shiny and stretchy.

Part Three of Three:
Dividing and Baking



1

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (180°C).



2

Divide the dough. Roll it out or press it into a circular shape, like a pizza crust. Use a knife to cut the dough in half, so you have two half-moons of dough.



3

Roll it up. Place one piece of dough in front of you so that one of the corners is pointing toward you. Pick up the corner and roll the dough away from your body, like you would a jelly roll. Roll it all the way up until you have a loaf. Repeat with the other piece of dough.If you don't want a rolled loaf shape, you can divide and shape the dough any way you want. Make traditional loaves, dinner rolls, pizza crust, or any other shape you like.



4

Make slits in the top. Use a knife to make a few slits in the top of each loaf. This helps the bread cook more evenly.



5

Place the unbaked bread on a baking sheet. A cookie sheet works great, or you could nestle it into a loaf pan.



6

Bake the bread for 30 minutes. It's ready when the top looks slightly golden brown. Serve it with butter and jam or alongside soups and stews.



7

Finished.

- 1 sdt pokak (sudah dihaluskan)

- 1/4 potong laos (sudah dihaluskan)

- 8 gr garam (sudah dilarutkan dengan air 4 L)

Langkah-langkah:

1) Siapkan semua alat dan bahan.

2) Awali dengan berdoa terlebih dahulu.

3) Cuci kedelai sampai bersih, lalu rendam selama 1x24 jam. Hal ini bertujuan agar kedelai menjadi lunak dan kulit arinya mudah terlepas.

4) Rebus kedelai hingga mendidih. Hal ini juga bertujuan agar kulit ari kedelai mudah terlepas.

5) Bilas kedelai dengan air bersih dan pastikan semua kulit ari kedelai sudah terlepas.

6) Tiriskan di tempat yang lebar hingga kedelai benar-benar dingin. Pastikan kedelai telah benar-benar dingin sempurna agar setiap ragi tempe yang nantinya ditebarkan tidak mati.

7) Taburkan ragi tempe ke kedelai yang sudah ditiriskan sambil diaduk agar ragi tempe tercampur rata ke seluruh kedelai.

8) Simpanlah kedelai yang telah dicampur dengan ragi tempe di ruangan yang bersuhu 25-30 °C. Lakukan penyimpanan ini selama 3-5 hari.

9) Setelah kedelai ditumbuhi jamur tempe, tambahkan larutan garam sebanyak 800 gr yang dicampur dengan 4 liter air bersih. Diamkan adonan kecap ini selama 3-4 minggu pada suhu ruangan (maksimal 2 bulan).

10) Setelah didiamkan, rebus campuran ini hingga mendidih kemudian saring dengan kain penyaring.

11) Rebus kembali hasil saringan adonan kecap tadi kemudian tambahkan bahan: 6 kg gula merah, 2 lembar daun salam, 1 lembar daun jeruk, 1 batang serai, 1 sdt pokak (sudah dihaluskan), dan 1/4 potong laos (sudah dihaluskan). Aduk perlahan agar gula merah mencair dan bahan-bahan lain juga tercampur rata.

12) Untuk menambahkan gula merah tergantung dari kebutuhan kita. Jika anda ingin membuat kecap manis maka anda membutuhkan 2 kg gula merah untuk setiap Liter sari adonan kecap yang direbus kembali pada langkah nomor 10. Berlaku kelipatannya.

13) Jika Anda hendak membuat kecap asin, Anda hanya membutuhkan 2,5 ons gula merah untuk setiap Liter sari adonan kecap yang direbus kembali. Berlaku kelipatannya.

14) Proses perebusan adonan kecap dihentikan bila adonan kecap sudah mendidih dan tidak ada buih lagi. Saring menggunakan kain saringan.

15) Kecap sudah siap untuk dikonsumsi dan dijadikan bumbu penyedap dalam setiap masakan Anda!
