9. soal ada difoto kalau kurang jelas tanyakan di komentar saja Keterangan soal:mapel: B. InggrisKelas: 7 SMP/MTssemester : 1model soal: isian​


Jawaban:

1. Diana never buy new dress. her beautiful dresses are given by her cousin.

2. They went to an amusement park with their family and friends

3. The children didn't come to their friend's birthday party

4. Tono and Toni go to school on foot everyday. Their little sister goes to school by buus because her school is so far away from the city

5. My brother likes playing guitar. he likes listening to music

Penjelasan:

karena kamu diminta untuk mengisi menggunakan possesive adjectives maka kamu bisa gunakan my, your, our, their, his, her, its.

possesive adjectives digunakan untuk menyatakan kepemilikan terhadap kata benda (noun).

contoh : i am looking for my pen. (pen : kata benda)

: is that your house? (house : kata benda)

untuk nomor 5, sepertinya harus diisi menggunakan subject pronouns ( i, you, we , they, he , she, it)

subject pronouns possesive adjectives

i = my

you = your

we = our

they = their

he = his

she = her

it = its

f1=4
f1+f2=8
f2=4
x=8
x2-x1=??
x=(x1f1+x2f2)/(f1+f2)
8=(7×4+ x2×4)/8
64=28+4 x2
36=4 x2
x2=36÷4=8
x2-x1=9-7=2
Misal
X1 : semester 1
X2: semester 2

Jadi,
X1 : 7,0 ✖ 4
X2 : a ✖ 4

P : 8,0

Jawaban:

Diketahui :

nilai semester 1 = 7,0

Nilai semester selama satu tahun = 8,0
