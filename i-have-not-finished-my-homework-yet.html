14. Respond this following statement by using should + verb ing.A.I have not finished my English task yet.B.A. I am very hungry now.B.​


MODAL "SHOULD-CONTINUOUS"

A. I have not finished my English task yet.

B. You should be finishing your English task soon. Your teacher will be angry.

A. I am very hungry now.

B. You should be eating some food. You will get stomachace.

EXPLANATION

~ Penggunaan Modal Aux. (Should) sebagai refleksi sebuah nasihat/pengajuan pendapat/rekomendasi

Rumus :

(+) S+Should+ main verb/base verb ..

(-) S+Should+not+main verb/base verb......

(?) Should+ S+main verb/base verb ........

Exp.

~ She should meet the dentist when she gets toothache.

~ He should wear the face mask to protect him from the coronavirus spreading.

~ Penggunaan Modal Aux. (Should) sebagai refleksk sebuah kewajiban (obligation) yang harus segera dilaksanakan.

Rumus :

(+) S+Should + be + verb-ing

(-) S+Should”+not+be+verb-ing

(?) Should+S+be+verb-ing

Exp.

~ You should be wearing the rain coat

~ She should be reading until the last chapter

15. She is so sleepy, but she _______ finished her homework yet.​


Has not > Hasn't

↪She is so sleepy, but she (hasn't) finished her homework yet.

PEMBAHASAN

Present Perfect Tense

↪Adalah suatu jenis kalimat yang membicarakan tentang hubungan antara aksi atau kegiatan yang terjadi pada masa lalu dengan masa kini. Kalimat pada present perfect juga kadang menggunakan adverb pada kalimatnya.

➡Rumus

(+) Subject + has/have + verb 3 + object

(-) Subject + has/have + not + verb 3 + object

(?) Has/have + subject + verb 3 + object + ?

➡KeteranganHas digunakan untuk subjek singular (he, she, it)Have digunakan untuk subjek plural (I, you, we, they)Penjelasan:

Kalau terdapat kata "yet" kemungkinan besar kalimat itu merupakan negatif dari Present Perfect tense.

39. I am doing my homework. The negative form of this sentence is?a. I am not did my homeworkb. I was not did my homeworkc. I am not doing my homeworkd. I am not do my homework


PART = GRAMMAR-PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSE-HOW TO FROM POSITIVE, NEGATIVE & INTERROGATIVE

-----------------

Jawaban:

C. Iam not doing my homework

Note :

Struktur kalimat diatas menerapkan present continuous tense. Bisa dicermati pada penggunaan kata kerja nya  BE  + Verb-ing.

Penjelasan:

PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSE Tenses ini digunakan untuk membahas aktifitas/tindakan yang sedang dilakukan ketika dibicarakan. Kata kerja nya pada umumnya ditambahkan akhiran -ing setelah tobe (is/am/are ) untuk menunjukkan/menggambarkan aktifitas sedang berlangsung sesaat ketika dibicarakan.Penggunaan keterangan waktu ( adverbs of time ) : now, right now, still, at the moment.

PATTERN

(+ ) SUBJECT+ TOBE ( IS/AM/ARE ) + VERB-ING + O/ COMPLEMENT

(- ) SUBJECT+TOBE( IS/AM/ARE)+NOT+VERB-ING+O/COMPLEMENT

(? ) TOBE(IS/AM/ARE)+SUBJECT+VERB-ING+O/COMPLEMENT ?

EXAMPLE :

(+ ) Iam doing my homework right now

( -) Iam not doing my homework right now

( ? ) Am I doing my homework right now?
