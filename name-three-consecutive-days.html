1. find three consecutive even numbers whose sum equals 180find three consecutive even numbers whose sum equals 180​


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Let's represent the three consecutive even numbers as x, x+2, and x+4.

The sum of these three numbers is 180, so we can write the equation:

x + (x+2) + (x+4) = 180

Now, let's solve the equation:

3x + 6 = 180

Subtract 6 from both sides:

3x = 174

Divide by 3:

x = 58

So, the first even number is 58. The next two consecutive even numbers are:

58 + 2 = 60

58 + 4 = 62

Therefore, the three consecutive even numbers whose sum equals 180 are 58, 60, and 62.
