32. my father likes to eat apple (-) (?)​


Jawaban:

My father likes to eat apple

(-) My father does not like to eat apple (?) Does My father like to eat apple?

Pembahasan :Simple Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yang dilakukan secara berulang, kebiasaan, rutin dan fakta umum.

Rumus Present Tense (+) S + Verb-1 (-s/es) (-) S + do/does + not + Verb-1(?) Do/does + S + Verb-1?

Catatan

Jika Subjek tunggal

kalimat positive menggunakan Verb+s/eskalimat negative dan interrogative menggunakan does

Jika Subjek jamak

kalimat positive menyatakan Verb1 tanpa tambahan -s/eskalimat negative dan interrogative menggunakan do

39. 18. My mother likes sugar in his coffee., my father likes black coffee.a. Neverthelessb. On the other handc. Unlessd. Althoughe. In contrast​


Jawaban:

My mother likes sugar in his coffee, although my father likes black coffee.

Bila diartikan: ibu saya menyukai gula di kopinya, meskipun ayah saya menyukai kopi hitam.

Penjelasan:

Yang harus diperhatikan pada kalimat ini adalah kata "his coffee", yang berarti kopinya (kopinya ayah).

Perhatikan kalimat berikut.

My mother likes sugar in her coffee. On the other hand, my father likes black coffee.

My mother likes white coffee. In contrast, my father likes black coffee.

My father likes black coffee nevertheless he likes sugar in his coffee.
