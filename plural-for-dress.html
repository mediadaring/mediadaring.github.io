(+) S+ to be (am/is/are) + V-ing + O

(-) S+ to be (am/is/are) + not + V-ing + O

27. show all of for you dress to them .in WITHOUT TO/FOR form is...a.show all of for you dress themb.show they all of your dressc.show them all of your dressd show all of your them dress​


Answer:

C

show them all of your dress

Explanation:

Show all of for you dress to them.

In terms of grammar, for and to can not be used in this sentence and the Pronounced for the people (which in the words) is used to Them not They.

(Above ↓ I used Bold/B for the wrong one and I for the correct one)

A. Show all of for you dress them

(1) (3) (4)(X)(Your*) (6) (2)

[Show all of for (You cannot use "for" in the word) you (your not "you") dress them (them should next to "show"] ✖ (Incorrect)

B. show they all of your dress

[show they (Them not "they") all of your dress] ✖ (Incorrect)

C. show them all of your dress

[show them all of your dress] ✔ (Correct)

D. show all of your them dress

[show all (all should be beside them) of your them (them should be beside "show") dress] ✖ (Incorrect)
