READING COMPREHENSION

1. Apa jenis teks tersebut?

Jawabannya : report text

➡ Ruang lingkup obyek yang dibahas adalah secara umum. Jadi, ini masuk kategori report text.

2. Apa deskripsi dari Kangguru?

Jawabannya :

~ Kangaroo usually eats grass and plants.

~ Kangaroos have short front legs, but very long very strong back legs and a tail.

~ Kangaroos can also run at speeds over 45 kilometers per hour

➡ Bisa ditemukan di bagian deskripsi paragraf kedua "Kangaroo usually eat grass and plants. They have short front legs, but very long very strong back legs and a tail. These a used for sitting up and for jumping. Kangaroo have been known to make forward jumps of over eight meters, and leap across fences more than three meters hight They can also run at speeds over 45 kilometers per hour."

Di situ dijelaskan mulai dari ciri fisik kangguru hingga tabiatnya.

3. Teksnya menjelaskan ke kita tentang apa?

Jawabannya : it tells about the description of kangaroo.

➡ Secara garis besar, report text berisi deskripsi/penjabaran rinci fenomena/tanaman/hewan/hal.

4. Teks tersebut ditulis untuk apa?

Jawabannya :

~ to describe about kangaroo in general.

~ to report the result of a research on kangaroo.

➡ Pada umumnya fungsi sosial report text adalah mendeskripsikan fenomena/hal/hewan secara umum atau melaporkan hasil observasi lapangan dari suatu hal.

5. Di mana kangguru tinggal?

Jawabannya : Kangaroo lives on Australian island of Tasmania and in New Guinea.

➡ Penjelasan ada di kalimat "A kangaroo is an animal found only in Australia. Althought it has a smaller relative, called a wallaby, which lives on the Australian island of Tasmania and also in New Guinea."

Di situ dijelasakan bahwa Kangguru tinggal di dataran Australia dan New Guinea.

6. Apa yang dimakan kangguru?

Jawabannya : Kangaroo eats grass and plants.

➡ Ada di kalimat "Kangaroo usually eat grass and plants."

Di situ dijelaskan bahwa kangguru biasanya makan rerumputan dan tanaman.

7. Kenapa kangguru disebut dengan hewan berkantung ?

Jawabannya : because the kangaroo especially female kangaroo has an external pouch on the front of her body.

➡ Ada di kalimat "Kangaroos are marsupials. This means that the female kangaroo has an extemal pouch on the front of her body."

Kangguru termasuk mamalia berkantung di mana kangguru betina memiliki kantung luar di bagian depan tubuhnya.

8. Apa sinonim dari kata "tiny?"

Jawabannya : small

➡ Kata "tiny" artinya kecil, mungil. Jadi, sinonimnya sama dengan kata "small."

9. Kata "it" merujuk ke apa ?

Jawabannya : It refers to a baby kangaroo.

➡ Kata rujukan bisa dipahami di penjelasan kalimat sebelumnya/awal kalimat "a baby kangaroo is very tiny when it is bom, and it.."

10. Apa sinonim dari kata "pouch?"

Jawabannya : pocket/sack

➡ Kata "pouch" memiliki arti kantung. Ini sama halnya dengan kata "pocket/sack."
