9. we were not welcome by the family because we ......(not make) a telephone call telling them that we would come last night.


finished action : past tense

... Did not make...

Semoga bermanfaatAnswer: 

The sentence above is clearly in past tense, based on the context and the grammar point. There are some words to indicate it such as 'were' and 'would'. So, the most possible answer for the gap is to make it in past tense. Yet, since it is expected to make a negative answer, you don't use V2 in it but, did not + V1. Here is the answer:

We were not welcome by the family because we didn't make a telephone call telling them that we would come last night.

hopefully that's clear enough. 

Kelas: SMA
Mapel: English
Kategori: past tense
