1. The Structure

The structure of curriculum vitae (CV) must be tidy and structured, so the other who read your CV could read it comfortly, also it will make the other sure you are seriously to take place in their company.

2. Color

The color of your CV must be considered too, as we know color can affect the reader, make sure your CV has some colors which make its looks better. Color can represent your creativity too.

3. Data

All data in your CV must be real without any manipulation, this is the most important thing of all. Re-check all data which you've entered into your CV, and make sure there is no mistake in your CV.

Rumus Present Perfect (+) S + has/have + V3 (-) S + has/have + not + V3 (?) Has/have + S + V3?

Catatan:Subject => My mother Has/have => hasVerb-3 => worked
