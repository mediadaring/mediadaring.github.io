Jawaban:

1. Through the song, the singer expresses her feeling that she is happy/joyful. This is evident from the repeated lines "Now I'm walking on sunshine, whoa I'm walking on sunshine, whoa I'm walking on sunshine, whoa And don't it feel good."

2. Based on the song lyrics, we know that the singer is hopeful or optimistic about reuniting with her lover. This is reflected in the lines "I used to think maybe you loved me, now, baby, I'm sure" and "And I just can't wait till the day when you knock on my door."

3. The singer used to feel uncertain or unsure about her lover's feelings. This is expressed in the lines "I used to think maybe you loved me, now I know that it's true" and "Now, I don't want you back for the weekend, not back for a day, no, no, no."

4. The word 'it' in the line "And don't it feel good" refers to the overall feeling of walking on sunshine and the joy expressed throughout the song.
