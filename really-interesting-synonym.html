6. this, that, these, those 1. Circle the right words. This / These homework is very difficult. That / Those shoes are really nice. That / These house is really old.These / This books aren't very interesting.​


Keterangan :

This dan that = digunakan untuk kata benda tunggal (hanya satu)

These dan those = digunakan untuk kata benda jamak (lebih dari satu)

(Kan disuruh dilingkari jadi dilingkarinya kata yang ditebelin ya)

Jawaban :

This / these homework is very difficult.

That / Those shoes are really nice.

That / These house is really old.

These / This books aren’t very insteresting.

Tambahan

untuk yang shoes (sepatu) itu those karena shoes (sepatu) itu 2 pasang, jadi lebih dari satu

This / These homework is very difficult. That / Those shoes are really nice.

That / Those house is really old.

These / This books aren't very interesting.

34. apa synonym dari had


Have, own, posses

Maaf kalau kurang nyambung

Question and answer :

What is the synonym of had?

Synonyms of had are possessed, owned, acquired, obtained, got, held, etc.

----------

Had adalah konjugasi dari kata kerja have yang berarti memiliki/mendapat.

Had adalah bentuk past participle  (v) dari have.

------------

Contoh kalimat yang menggunakan kata had dan sinonimnya.

Tom had an A for his English test.

Tom got an A for his English test.

Mr Smith had an old car.

Mr Smith owned an old car.

Mr Smith possessed an old car.

He had a key in his hand.

He held a key in his hand.

I had the painting at an auction.

I obtained the painting at an auction.

I acquired the painting at an auction.
