Passive sentences are the sentences which focuses on the objects instead of the subject at the beginning of it. The other functions are to state the process, changes, etc. 
The pattern is O + auxiliary verb + V3 + by + S

Jawaban:

a. The students' behaviour is observed by the teacher every day.

b. Vacation is being needed by the workers to make their minds relax.

c. Babies' parents' behaviour was imitated by babies.*

d. The patient who get mental illness will be interviewed by psychologist.

*notes

"their parents'" berarti "orang tua mereka"

mereka (their) mengacu pada babies. meletakkan their di awal kalimat tanpa referensi atau acauan sebelumnya akan terdengar tidak pas. maka, their di awal kalimat diganti menjadi babies'.

Jawaban:

a. Student's behavior is observed by teacher everyday

b. Vacation is being needed by workers to make their mind relax

c. Their parent's behaviour was imitated by babies

d. The patient who get mental illness will be interviewed by Psychologist

Penjelasan:

Passive voice mengubah subjek menjadi objek dan objek menjadi subject.

1 a. Teacher observes students’ behaviour everyday

menjadi :

Student's behavior is observed by teacher everyday

Simple present tense:

S + V1 + O/C

menjadi :

O + tobe (is,am,are) + V3 + S

2. b. Vacation is being needed by workers to make their mind relax

menjadi :

b. Vacation is being needed by workers to make their mind relax

Present perfect tense

S + tobe(is,am,are) + V1 + ing + O/C

menjadi :

O + tobe(is,am,are) + being + V3 +S

3. c. Babies imitated their parents’ behaviour

menjadi :

c. Their parent's behaviour was imitated by babies

Simple past tense:

S + V2 + O/C

menjadi :

O + tobe(was/were) + V3 + S

4.d. Psychologist will interview the patient who get mental illness

menjadi :

d. The patient who get mental illness will be interviewed by Psychologist

Simple future tense

S + will + V1 + O/C

menjadi :

O + will be + V3 + S

Present perfect tense tells about the activity which is begun in the past and it will be continued and finished recently.

Present perfect tense also discusses the experience.

Our boss ____ (make) the decision yet when management changed their mind.

Dari statement diatas, ada petunjuk dari kata "yet" Di akhir klausa utama ( main clause ). Ini menunjukkan untuk pernyataan bentuk negatif dalam present perfect tense.

Berikut pola present perfect tense :

S+has/have+not+verb3+object......

So , the correct answer is

Our boss hasn't made the decision yet when management changed their mind.
