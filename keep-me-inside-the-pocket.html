Amanda's pocket money : Brian's pocket money = 4 : 7

Brian's pocket money : Candy's pocket money = 3 : 5

Therefore, the ratio of Amanda's pocket money to Candy's pocket money is:

Amanda's pocket money : Candy's pocket money = (4 * 3) : (7 * 5)

= 12 : 35
