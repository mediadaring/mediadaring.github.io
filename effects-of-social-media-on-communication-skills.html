11. 1.doscuss the impact of social media on society2.explain the conceft of globalization and its effcets on the economy​


Jawaban:

1. **Impact of Social Media on Society:**

Social media has brought about significant changes in how society communicates, interacts, and consumes information. Some of its impacts include:

- **Communication:** Social media platforms enable instant global communication, connecting people across geographical boundaries. This has fostered the spread of ideas, cultures, and news on an unprecedented scale.

- **Information Dissemination:** Information now spreads rapidly through social media, both accurate and misleading. This challenges traditional media's monopoly on news distribution and requires critical thinking skills to discern credible sources.

- **Community Building:** Social media offers platforms for individuals to find and connect with like-minded people, forming communities based on shared interests, beliefs, or identities.

- **Business and Marketing:** Businesses utilize social media for marketing and customer engagement. Influencers and user-generated content impact consumer behavior and shape trends.

- **Privacy Concerns:** The digital footprint left on social media raises privacy concerns, as personal information becomes vulnerable to misuse or exploitation.

- **Mental Health:** Excessive social media use can contribute to issues like anxiety, depression, and low self-esteem. Comparisons with curated online personas may lead to unrealistic expectations.

- **Spread of Misinformation:** False information can spread quickly, causing real-world consequences. Social media platforms struggle to combat misinformation effectively.

- **Political Influence:** Social media has become a platform for political discussions, mobilization, and activism. It can amplify political movements and change public opinion.

2. **Concept of Globalization and its Effects on the Economy:**

Globalization refers to the increasing interconnectedness of economies, cultures, and societies worldwide. It has had profound effects on the economy, including:

- **Trade and Investment:** Globalization has facilitated the movement of goods, services, and capital across borders. Trade agreements and reduced tariffs have increased international commerce.

- **Labor Market:** Globalization has led to outsourcing and offshoring, where companies seek cost-effective labor and resources in other countries. This has impacted employment patterns globally.

- **Technology Transfer:** Globalization promotes the exchange of technology, ideas, and innovation between countries, accelerating technological advancements.

- **Multinational Corporations:** Globalization has given rise to multinational corporations that operate in multiple countries. They contribute to economic growth but can also exploit resources and labor.

- **Cultural Exchange:** The exchange of ideas and cultural practices occurs due to globalization. This can lead to cultural enrichment but also cultural homogenization.

- **Inequality:** Globalization can exacerbate income inequality both within and between countries. Developed economies may benefit more than developing ones.

- **Financial Interdependence:** Globalization has increased financial interdependence, making economies more vulnerable to economic shocks and crises in other parts of the world.

- **Environmental Impact:** Globalization can lead to environmental challenges due to increased resource consumption, pollution, and carbon emissions associated with global trade.

Overall, globalization has transformed economies by opening up opportunities and challenges, shaping policies, and requiring adaptation to a more interconnected world.

Jawaban:

1. When expressing our goals, we often use expressions such as:

- "My goal is to..."

- "I aim to..."

- "I'm striving to..."

- "I'm determined to..."

- "I'm committed to..."

- "I have set my sights on..."

- "I have a clear objective to..."

2. The material of expressing intention, agreement, and goals can have a significant impact on communication skills. It helps foster clarity, assertiveness, and effective communication. By expressing intentions clearly, we can ensure that our message is understood, and others are aware of what we are aiming for. Agreement language helps build consensus and ensures that all parties involved are on the same page. Goals provide direction, motivation, and a shared sense of purpose, making communication more focused and productive.

3. The material of expressing intention, agreement, and goals can benefit social skills in several ways:

- It promotes effective collaboration and teamwork by ensuring that everyone's intentions, agreements, and goals are aligned.
