The correct sequence of producing Faber-Castell Erasure is as follows:

1. Raw Material Acquisition: The process begins with the acquisition of raw materials required for making erasers, such as rubber and other additives.

2. Mixing and Formulation: The raw materials are mixed and formulated in specific ratios to achieve the desired properties and consistency of the eraser compound.

3. Compounding: The compound is then processed further using specialized equipment to create a homogeneous mixture and ensure proper dispersion of additives.

4. Molding: The compound is molded into the desired eraser shape using molds or extrusion techniques. The molds may have various designs or sizes depending on the intended use of the eraser.

5. Curing: The molded erasers are then subjected to a curing process, which involves applying heat or chemical treatments to solidify and stabilize the eraser material.

6. Quality Control: The erasers undergo thorough quality control checks to ensure they meet the required standards in terms of performance, appearance, and durability.

7. Packaging: The approved erasers are packaged into suitable packaging materials, which may include individual wrappers or bulk packaging, depending on the intended market.

8. Distribution: The packaged erasers are then distributed to wholesalers, retailers, or directly to consumers through various channels.

It's important to note that the specific manufacturing process for Faber-Castell erasers may vary based on their specific product lines and variations. The above sequence provides a general overview of the typical steps involved in producing erasers.

27. Which sentences is the correct from of passive?


Manakah kalimat passive yang benar/tepat?

Jawabannya :

38). (A) lots of plants from Amazon HAVE BEEN TESTED as medicine.

Alasan :

Dalam kalimat pasive, kata kerja nya harus bentuk V3. Dan sebelum V3 harus ada To be atau pun auxiliary verb terlebih dahulu (tergantung tenses yang dipakai). Dan untuk nomor 38 ini tenses yang digunakan dalam kalimat passive nya adalah PRESENT PERFECT TENSE. Dimana rumus nya :

Active : S + Have/has + V3 + O

Passive : S + Have/has + been + V3 + by..

39). (C) some medications HAVE already BEEN successfully PRODUCED from rainforest plants.

Alasan :

=> Tenses yang di pakai masih menggunakan PRESENT PERFECT TENSE seperti yang digunakan pada nomor 38.

33. complete the following sentences with the correct forms of verbs​


Jawaban :StartedLedEncouragedBeganSetPEMBAHASANSIMPLE PAST

➡ Tenses yang menjelaskan tentang suatu kegiatan ataupun kejadian yang terjadi pada masa lampau.

RUMUS :

(+) S + V2 + O

They opened the door. She swept the floor.

(-) S + Did + not + V1 + O

They did not open the door. She did not sweep the floor.

(?) Did + S + V1 + O

Did they open the door? Did she sweep the floor?

Pada soal diatas kita diperintahkan untuk mengubah kata kerjanya menggunakan regular/irregular verbs. Karena kalimatnya bentuk lampau dan kalimatnya bentuk positif semua, maka kata kerjanya harus di ubah ke bentuk verb-2, dan berikut adalah perubahannya :

Regular Verbs

➡ Kata kerja beraturan, dimana bentuk verb-2 dan ketiganya tidak jauh berbeda dari kata aslinya (verb-1) karena hanya tinggal menambahkan -d/ed.

Start => Started => StartedEncourage => Encouraged => Encouraged

Irregular Verbs

➡ Kata kerja tidak beraturan, dimana bentuk verb-2 dan verb-3 nya sudah berbeda dari kata aslinya (verb-1)

Lead => Led => LedBegin => Began => BegunSet => Set => Set
