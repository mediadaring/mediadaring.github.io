25. Tony telah mengikuti beberapa Try Out matematika yang diadakan oleh gurunya disekolah. Diketahui bahwa masih ada satu try out lagi yang harus diikuti Tony. Jika Tony mendapatkan nilai 80 pada try out berikutnya, rata-rata try out Tony adalah 85. Sedangkan jika Tony mendapatkan nilai 95 pada try out berikutnya, rata-ratanya menjadi 88. Berapa banyak jumlah Try Out seluruhnya yang diikuti Tony ?


anggap aja

n = bnyak tryout

x = jmlah nilai tryout sebelumnya

berarti :

Xn + 80 / (n+1) = 85..

Xn + 80 = 85n + 85

Xn = 85n + 5...(1)

Xn + 95 /(n+1) = 88

Xn + 95 = 88n + 88

Xn = 88n - 7...(2)

eliminasi deh.

Xn = 85n + 5

Xn = 88n - 7

---------------------- -

0 = -3n + 12

-3n = -12

n = 12/3

n = 4

berarti tony 4 x ngikut tryout..

   The story starts off with a boy named Thomas who wakes up in a metal box, he doesn't remember anything about his past or anything at all, except his name. When he steps out of the metal box he finds himself in a strange place called the glade. There are about 50 other boys who've come to the glade who also don't know about there past, who called themselves the gladers. He then meets the leader of the gladers, a boy named Alby and another boy named Newt. Then there's Chuck, the last "newbie" to come to the glade who becomes good friends with Thomas, at the time, the only friend he had.

   As time goes by, Newt explains why the glade was there and what its purpose was. The Glade is in the shape of a perfect square forming a box around the gladers, and outside the glade is the maze. There are keepers who are in charge of certain things around the glade. The most important people in the glade are the runners, the ones who go out running through the maze. The Gladers' number one goal, is to solve the maze, and find their way home.

   Their is one threat stopping them from figuring out the maze, one threat stopping them from going out at night and from going too far into the maze, horrific animals that go by the names of grievers. Giant bug-like creatures that are strongest at night. Thomas, on his first day, finds out that if get stung by a griever you go through a process called the changing. As Thomas's experience continues, he starts feel as if the glade is somewhat familiar, as if he's been there before, and out of the blue has a sudden urge to become a runner. Then things start to change.

Not only did the person come earlier than usual, but the new glader was a girl. At first the girl looked dead, but was discovered that she was in a coma. Alby said they should go check it out and see what might have caused it, but when tried to come back, Minho was pulling him, and Alby wasn't moving. Alby was stung by one of the grievers, and Thomas decides to go out and try to help him, but they all end up stuck in the maze at night.

   Minho ran away, completely abandoning Thomas and Alby who was still unconscious. Thomas then tried to climb the wall, carrying Alby as well, he made progress by getting to about 30 feet up the wall, but then grievers started to come. Thomas was forced to leave Alby on the wall, and had to run, or swing, for his life. He out smarted a couple grievers, and when he found Minho again, they outsmarted all of the grievers who came towards them into falling off a cliff. They ended up surviving the night, the first people ever to survive the night, even Alby was still alive.

   The next morning the new girl woke up at last, and has a special gift, she can talk to Thomas in his mind. Then things start to fall apart, the screen turns grey as if it were a screen, and on that night, the gates did not close. The grievers had complete access to the glade, and attacked it. During the attack a boy named Gally came to a place where a few gladers were hiding and said that the grievers would kill one glader every night. After three days, three gladers died, and that's when Thomas came up with a terrible idea. He would have to Get stung by a griever on purpose and go through the changing,get his memories.

   Thomas's idea was a success and finds out very bad things about him and Teresa. Thomas and Teresa helped create the glade and helped come up with the idea. He also finds out about strange things like the glade isn't outdoor, and that the gladers' names aren't even their real names. Then he remembered the most important part, how to get out. They would have to go through the griever hole, the home of the grievers.

   Later that day Thomas and Teresa find a code hidden in the maps of the maze. That code was used to escape the maze and kill every single griever there. Half of the gladers died that day, fighting for their lives against the grievers. When they came face to face with the creators Thomas noticed that Gally was there and he was forced to try and kill Thomas, and chuck sacrificed himself to save Thomas. then rescuers bursted through the door killing the creators and saved all of the remaining gladers. They now live in a world with the most  horrible disease known to mankind. The Flare.
