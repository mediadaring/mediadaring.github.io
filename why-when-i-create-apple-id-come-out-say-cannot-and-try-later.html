7. when will you say thanks ? and why you do that​


Jawaban:

1. When a leader's words have changed your thinking and your life for the better

In the internet age, it is easy for us to remain anonymous following someone's teachings. However, few "thank you" are spoken when the thoughts of such great leaders or figures succeed in changing your life for the better.

2. When someone compliments you

Whenever people give us compliments, we are often reluctant to respond to those words. In fact, we tend to discourage ourselves and make these words of praise a form of sarcasm. However, accepting compliments is not a sign of arrogance when the word "thank you" is returned.

3. When someone gives you a suggestion

A person's advice whether asked for or not is a form of concern. Even if you decide whether the advice is useful or not, it shows that they are trying to help. Instead of rejecting a suggestion, try to think of it as kind and say "thank you."

4. Everyone helps provide solutions

As a social being, one of the human desires is to play a role in the lives of others. Many people want to be involved and help, but few are aware of it. You may think right now that everything can be done alone, but never underestimate the help of others. Try to be brave enough to say "thank you" when someone wants to help you.

n = bnyak tryout

x = jmlah nilai tryout sebelumnya

berarti :

Xn + 80 / (n+1) = 85..

Xn + 80 = 85n + 85

Xn = 85n + 5...(1)

Xn + 95 /(n+1) = 88

Xn + 95 = 88n + 88

Xn = 88n - 7...(2)

eliminasi deh.

Xn = 85n + 5

Xn = 88n - 7

---------------------- -

0 = -3n + 12

-3n = -12

n = 12/3

n = 4
