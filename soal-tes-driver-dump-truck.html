A pedestrian who saw the incident said that the fuel truck was seen speeding then all of sudden swerved and hit a motorcyclist who came from the front on 1st December 2014. This incident made the motorcycle rider was killed instantly after being hit by the truck while the truck driver suffered serious injuries to the chest and head. 30 minutes after the incident, the police came to investigate the Accident. After doing investigation, they found the cause that the driver was sleepy when driving the truck. In addition to the brake that didn’t work, so he couldn’t control his truck and hit the victim.
